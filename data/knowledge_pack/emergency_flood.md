# Emergency Flood Playbook

source_id: local:flood

## purpose
Provide safe first actions and planning guidance for street flooding, rising water, and flood-adjacent hazards.

## signs
- Rising or fast-moving water.
- Water covering roads, drains, outlets, or utility equipment.
- People trapped, unable to leave, or unsure about safe routes.

## immediate_actions
- Move to higher ground away from floodwater.
- Keep people away from water that may hide drains, debris, or energized objects.
- Use official evacuation routes when available.

## avoid
- Do not walk or drive through floodwater.
- Do not touch submerged electrical equipment, wires, poles, or vehicles.
- Do not re-enter unsafe areas to collect belongings.

## when_to_call_emergency_services
- Call local emergency services if anyone is trapped, injured, swept by water, near electricity, or unable to evacuate safely.

## calm_mode_guidance
- Map repeat flood locations and blocked access routes.
- Prepare family/community meeting points and check-in procedures.
- Pre-stage non-contact warning signs, detour plans, and utility contact lists.

## responder_packet_fields
- water_depth_estimate
- road_access_status
- trapped_people_unknown
- visible_utilities
- evacuation_route_status

## safety_notes
- MaydaIQ cannot verify current water speed or hidden hazards.
- Official evacuation and utility guidance overrides app output.

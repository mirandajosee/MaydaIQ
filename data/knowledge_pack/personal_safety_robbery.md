# Personal Safety And Robbery Playbook

source_id: local:personal_safety_robbery

## purpose
Prioritize escape, distance, and official help for robbery, violence, or personal safety threats.

## signs
- Threat, robbery, assault, weapon mention, stalking, intimidation, or unsafe person nearby.
- Victim or witness is still close to the unsafe area.

## immediate_actions
- Move to a safe, public, well-lit place.
- Stay with trusted people and keep distance from the threat.
- Contact local emergency services if there is violence, weapon risk, injury, or immediate danger.

## avoid
- Do not confront, pursue, follow, or detain anyone.
- Do not share names, faces, plates, or suspect identities in the app.
- Do not return to recover items while the area may be unsafe.

## when_to_call_emergency_services
- Call local emergency services for immediate danger, violence, weapons, injuries, threats, or an unsafe person nearby.

## calm_mode_guidance
- Document non-identifying facts once safe: time, approximate location, what happened, and support needed.
- Improve lighting, buddy systems, safe waiting areas, and reporting paths.

## responder_packet_fields
- immediate_danger
- injury_possible
- safe_callback_context
- approximate_location
- non_identifying_timeline

## safety_notes
- MaydaIQ never identifies suspects or private individuals.
- Safety and official response are more important than recovering property.

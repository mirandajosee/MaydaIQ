# MaydaIQ Safety Policy

source_id: local:safety_policy

## purpose
Set guardrails for crisis guidance, privacy, uncertainty, and simulated reporting.

## signs
- High severity, low confidence, missing emergency escalation, dangerous instruction, identity risk, or unsupported certainty.

## immediate_actions
- Prioritize physical safety and distance from hazards.
- Include confidence and uncertainty notes in every response.
- Trigger human escalation for high severity or low confidence.

## avoid
- Do not claim to contact authorities.
- Do not give confrontation, pursuit, vigilantism, unsafe re-entry, or invasive medical instructions.
- Do not identify people, faces, license plates, suspects, or private individuals.

## when_to_call_emergency_services
- Tell users to contact local emergency services for life risk, fire, injury, violence, floodwater, electricity, or immediate danger.

## calm_mode_guidance
- Use citations from Foundry IQ or local knowledge.
- Separate observations from conclusions.
- Make unknowns explicit and keep recommendations conservative.

## responder_packet_fields
- human_escalation_required
- confidence
- uncertainty_notes
- privacy_redactions
- simulated_only
- safety_review_status

## safety_notes
- MaydaIQ is not an emergency service.
- All dispatch/reporting behavior is simulated only.
- Human responders and official guidance override app output.

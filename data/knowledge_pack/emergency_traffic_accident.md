# Traffic Accident Playbook

source_id: local:traffic_accident

## purpose
Support safe bystander and responder-ready reporting for crashes, blocked roads, and possible injuries.

## signs
- Vehicle collision, blocked lane, debris, leaking fluids, fire risk, or injured person possible.
- People standing near traffic or secondary crash risk.

## immediate_actions
- Move yourself and others away from traffic if it is safe.
- Keep injured people still unless there is immediate danger such as fire.
- Share concise location and hazard details with local emergency services when injury or danger is present.

## avoid
- Do not stand in travel lanes.
- Do not provide invasive medical care.
- Do not move injured people unless they face immediate greater danger.

## when_to_call_emergency_services
- Call local emergency services for injury, trapped occupants, fire/smoke, leaking fuel, blocked major roads, or unsafe traffic conditions.

## calm_mode_guidance
- Identify dangerous intersections, poor lighting, recurring collision patterns, and detour options.
- Prepare reporting templates for road crews and traffic control.

## responder_packet_fields
- vehicle_count
- injury_possible
- blocked_lanes
- fire_or_fluid_hazard
- traffic_direction_impacted

## safety_notes
- Bystanders should not become additional casualties.
- MaydaIQ cannot assess injuries from a report or image.
